FAQ

Answers that remove friction before reservation.

Do you accept same-day reservations?

Yes. Same-day reservations are accepted until 10:30 PM, subject to table availability. WhatsApp confirmations are usually sent within 10 minutes during open hours.

Can I order food directly on WhatsApp?

Yes. Tap any WhatsApp order button and our team will confirm availability, delivery radius, payment link, and dispatch time.

Do you offer private dining?

Yes. The Maharaja Room hosts 12-24 guests, while full buyouts are available for 25-80 guests with fixed menus and event support.

Are vegetarian and Jain options available?

Yes. The menu includes vegetarian signatures, and Jain preparations can be arranged with advance notice.
